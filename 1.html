About NIR8 Calculators

Free online pharmaceutical dispensing calculator for pharmacists and chemists. Formula Calculator supports F1, F2, F3 assay-based formulas with water content correction. The (A+C)−B Calculator tracks standard quantities across up to four lots. No download needed — works directly in your browser with instant results and printable calculations.

Features: Assay correction, water content adjustment, quantity tracking, lot management, printable calculations. Ideal for pharmaceutical dispensing, quality control, and laboratory calculations.

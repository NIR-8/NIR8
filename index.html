About NIR8 Calculators – Free Online Pharmaceutical Calculator

NIR8 Calculators is a free online tool designed for pharmaceutical dispensing and batch calculation. Use it online directly in your browser — no download or installation needed.

The Formula Calculator supports three assay-based formulas: F1, F2, and F3. The (A+C)−B Calculator tracks standard quantities across up to four lots.

About NIR8 Calculators – Free Online Pharmaceutical Calculator

NIR8 Calculators is a free online tool for pharmaceutical dispensing and batch calculation. Formula Calculator supports F1, F2, F3 assay-based formulas. The (A+C)−B Calculator tracks standard quantities across up to four lots.

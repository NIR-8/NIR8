Precision Tools for Pharmaceutical Dispensing

Our Mission

NIR8 Calculators was developed to streamline the complex calculations involved in pharmaceutical dispensing and batch processing. Our goal is to provide pharmaceutical professionals with a free, reliable, and web-based suite of tools that minimize manual errors and improve workflow efficiency.

Core Features

Formula Calculator

Execute assay-based calculations (F1, F2, and F3) with built-in support for water content and assay corrections.

Lot Management

The (A+C)-B calculator allows for precise tracking across up to four different lots, ensuring standard quantities are maintained.

Accessibility

No downloads required. Optimized for browser use with keyboard shortcuts for rapid data entry and printing.

Reliability & Precision

In a regulated environment, accuracy is paramount. NIR8 Calculators are designed with logic that flags quantity mismatches automatically. While our tools are rigorously tested, we encourage users to cross-verify results as part of their standard Quality Assurance protocols.
